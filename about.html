What We Are
Behavioral Intelligence for Traders

Unlike ordinary trading journals, MindOfTrader focuses on the psychology behind trading decisions — including revenge trading, overtrading, fear-based exits, rule violations, and setup inconsistency.

The platform is designed to turn raw trading data into practical self-awareness through behavioral analytics, AI-powered coaching, and personalized trading DNA insights.

The Problem We Solve
Why Traders Keep Losing Despite Good Strategies

Most trading education focuses on charts, indicators, and strategies. But the research is clear — for most retail traders, behavioral factors account for a larger share of losses than strategy failures do.

Revenge trading after losses. Chasing breakouts out of FOMO. Exiting winners early out of fear. Adding to losing positions. Overtrading on bad days. These patterns repeat because they're invisible in a standard P&L statement.

MindOfTrader makes these patterns visible, attaches rupee costs to each behavior, and gives traders the tools to break these cycles — starting with self-awareness, backed by data.

Who It's Built For
Indian Options Traders Who Take Trading Seriously

MindOfTrader is specifically designed for NIFTY and BANKNIFTY options traders in India — where intraday options trading is widespread and behavioral pressure (FOMO, revenge trading, overtrading) is intense due to high leverage and fast price action.

Whether you're a full-time trader, a semi-professional trading alongside a job, or a serious hobbyist trying to improve consistency — MindOfTrader gives you a data mirror that reflects your actual decision-making, not your ideal one.
