About
Varsha Singh — Founder of MindOfTrader

Varsha Singh is the founder of MindOfTrader, a behavioral intelligence platform for traders.

MindOfTrader was created to help traders go beyond simple profit-and-loss tracking and understand the deeper behavioral patterns that shape performance. The platform focuses on trading psychology, discipline analysis, emotional control, behavioral mistake detection, and AI-assisted self-review.

By combining journaling, analytics, trading behavior insights, and coaching-style feedback, MindOfTrader is designed to help traders develop consistency, self-awareness, and stronger decision-making habits.

Why MindOfTrader Exists
The Gap Most Traders Never See

Many traders spend years improving strategies while ignoring the behavioral patterns that repeatedly damage results. MindOfTrader was built to close that gap — by helping traders measure and improve discipline, emotional control, and execution quality.

The insight is simple but powerful: a trader with an average strategy and excellent emotional discipline will consistently outperform a trader with a great strategy and poor behavioral control. Most tools track the strategy. MindOfTrader tracks the trader.

India's options markets are particularly high-pressure environments — fast-moving, high-leverage, emotionally intense. Behavioral patterns that cost a few thousand rupees in one session can compound into lakhs of losses over a year. MindOfTrader exists to make those patterns visible before they become irreversible.

The Platform
What MindOfTrader Does

MindOfTrader is a single behavioral intelligence platform that gives traders five integrated tools: a real-time behavioral guard that intercepts revenge and FOMO trades before execution, an AI coach powered by Claude that analyzes behavioral patterns across trade history, a smart journal that captures the psychology behind every decision, a weekly Trader DNA report that profiles behavioral archetypes and blind spots, and a behavioral analytics dashboard that scores discipline, patience, risk control, and emotional stability.

The platform supports direct connection to Zerodha via API and CSV import from all major Indian brokers including Dhan, Angel, and others.
