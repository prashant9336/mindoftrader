How to Export CSV — Step by Step
Z
Zerodha Kite
Trade Book CSV · Recommended format
1
Open kite.zerodha.com → Click your name (top right) → Console
2
Left sidebar → Reports → Trade Book
3
Select date range (up to 1 year) → Click ⬇ Download button → Save as CSV
4
Upload the file above ↑ — we detect Zerodha format automatically and import all NIFTY/BANKNIFTY options trades
✅ Columns detected: symbol, trade_type, price, quantity, trade_date
D
Dhan
Trade History CSV
1
Open web.dhan.co → Click Portfolio in top menu
2
Click Trade History → Select date range
3
Click Export → Download as CSV → Upload above
4
Dhan exports: symbol, side, price, qty, trade_date
A
Angel One (SmartAPI)
Orderbook CSV
1
Login to trade.angelone.in → Click your profile
2
Go to Reports → Trade Book
3
Select FO segment → date range → Click Download CSV
4
Angel exports: tradingsymbol, transaction_type, average_price, quantity
U
Upstox
Trade History CSV
1
Login to pro.upstox.com → Top menu → Portfolio
2
Trade History → Select FO segment → Choose date range
3
Click Download → Select CSV format → Upload above
4
Upstox exports: symbol, side, avg_price, qty, trade_date
I
ICICI Direct / Kotak / HDFC
Order History CSV
1
Login to your broker portal → Go to Reports or Order History
2
Filter by FO / Derivatives segment → Select date range (max 90 days usually)
3
Download as CSV or Excel → Upload above
4
If import fails, try renaming columns to: symbol, trade_type, price, quantity, trade_date
🏦
Any Other Broker
Generic CSV format
For any broker not listed above, your CSV needs at least these 4 columns (names can vary — we auto-detect):
symbol  |  buy/sell  |  price  |  quantity  |  date
1
Export your trade/order history as CSV from your broker
2
Open in Excel or Google Sheets — verify it has symbol, trade type, price, quantity, and date columns
3
Upload above — if 0 trades detected, rename the columns to match: symbol, trade_type, price, quantity, trade_date
Z
Zerodha API
Direct connect · Auto-sync
Skip CSV entirely — connect directly via Zerodha API for automatic trade import.
