How It Works

From Trade Data to Self-Awareness.

1
Connect Your Broker or Upload CSV
Link Zerodha via API or drag-drop any broker's trade export. MindOfTrader auto-detects options trades and organizes them by date, instrument, and session.
2
Log the Psychology Behind Each Trade
Tag each trade with your emotion state, rule adherence, setup quality, and intent. The journal captures not just what you traded — but why.
3
AI Analyzes Your Behavioral Patterns
Claude AI scans your full history and identifies your top behavioral mistakes, their frequency, and their rupee cost — ranked by impact.
4
Guard Intervenes in Real Time
As you log trades during a session, the behavioral guard monitors for revenge, FOMO, and overtrading patterns — and alerts you before you act on them.
5
Review Your Weekly Trading DNA
Every week, get your personalized DNA report — your behavioral archetype, edge, blind spots, and one high-impact thing to fix this week.
