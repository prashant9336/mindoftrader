Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know.

What exactly is MindOfTrader?▼
MindOfTrader is a behavioral trading intelligence platform for Indian options traders. It tracks not just what you traded, but why — your emotional state, rule adherence, setup quality — and uses AI to find the specific behavioral patterns costing you the most money.
How is this different from a regular trading journal?▼
A regular journal records what happened. MindOfTrader explains why it happened and how much it cost you. It attaches a rupee cost to every behavioral mistake, scores your discipline in real time, uses AI to find hidden patterns, and generates a shareable weekly behavioral DNA report.
Is MindOfTrader completely free?▼
Yes — completely free to start, no credit card required. Full access to journal, AI analysis, behavioral dashboard, and DNA report. Premium features (multi-account, auto-sync, coaching plans) are coming — join the waitlist above for early access.
Does it work with Zerodha?▼
Yes. Connect via Zerodha's API for direct import, or download your trade history as CSV from Kite → Console → Reports → Trade Book and upload it. We also support Dhan, Angel One, Upstox, and any broker that exports trade history as CSV.
Is my trade data safe?▼
Your trade data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and is not sent to any server. The AI analysis uses anonymized behavioral summaries. We have read-only access to broker APIs — we cannot place or modify any orders.
Who is MindOfTrader built for?▼
Built specifically for Indian NIFTY and BANKNIFTY options traders — full-time, semi-professional, or serious part-time. Anyone who has ever revenge-traded, chased a breakout, exited a winner too early, or added to a losing position will immediately recognize the value.
How quickly can I see results?▼
The AI analysis runs instantly after you import trades. Most traders notice behavioral insights within their first session. Measurable improvement in win rate typically shows within 30 days of consistent use — based on self-reported data from early users.
